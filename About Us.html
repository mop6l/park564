About Us

Our Story

The 564 Park Avenue Preservation Foundation was established in 1985 by a group of passionate architects, historians, and local residents who recognized the need to protect and preserve one of New York City's most iconic architectural landmarks. Our journey began with a simple mission: to safeguard the architectural integrity and historical significance of 564 Park Avenue for future generations.

Over the years, we have grown from a small, grassroots organization into a respected institution in the field of architectural preservation. Our efforts have not only helped maintain the splendor of 564 Park Avenue but have also contributed to the broader conversation about the importance of preserving New York's architectural heritage.

Our Mission

At the 564 Park Avenue Preservation Foundation, our mission is multifaceted:

  • To preserve and restore the architectural features of 564 Park Avenue
  • To educate the public about the historical and cultural significance of the building
  • To advocate for policies that protect architectural landmarks in New York City
  • To foster a community of individuals passionate about architectural preservation
  • To implement sustainable practices in building maintenance and restoration

Our Achievements

Since our inception, we have:

  • Successfully restored the building's facade to its original 1920s grandeur
  • Established a comprehensive educational program that reaches over 5,000 students annually
  • Secured landmark status for three additional buildings in the Park Avenue historic district
  • Implemented state-of-the-art sustainable technologies in building maintenance
  • Raised over $10 million for preservation efforts through our annual gala and community initiatives
