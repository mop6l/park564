Our Mission

The 564 Park Avenue Preservation Foundation is dedicated to safeguarding the architectural integrity and historical significance of one of New York City's most treasured landmarks. Through education, advocacy, and careful restoration efforts, we aim to ensure that this iconic building continues to stand as a testament to the city's rich architectural heritage for generations to come.

Our Preservation Efforts

Architectural Restoration

We meticulously restore and maintain the building's original architectural features, preserving its historic charm and structural integrity.

Art Conservation

Our team of experts works tirelessly to preserve and restore the building's valuable artwork and decorative elements.

Sustainable Practices

We implement eco-friendly solutions to ensure the building's longevity while minimizing its environmental impact.

Educational Programs

We offer various educational initiatives to raise awareness about architectural preservation and its importance to our cultural heritage.

Historical Research

Our dedicated team conducts in-depth research to uncover and document the rich history of 564 Park Avenue and its significance in New York's architectural landscape.

Community Engagement

We actively involve the local community in our preservation efforts through workshops, events, and volunteer programs, fostering a sense of shared responsibility for our architectural heritage.
